Alexis Ohanian (Co-Founder of Reddit) on "Without Their Permission"

Rose Auditorium - October 5th, 6 pm

Alexis Ohanian visits the Cooper Union on the first stop of a national book tour for his new book Without Their Permission. Alexis will be talking about his journey as an entrepreneur and how the internet is "the most powerful and democratic tool for disseminating information in human history," and will engage Qwiki Co-Founder Doug Imbruce in a fireside chat about the potential in internet entrepreneurship. The event will be followed by pizza and a book signing by Alexis!
